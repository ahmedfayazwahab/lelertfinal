Meet Our Chefs

Chef Art

​Chef Prasopchok Trakulphat, known as Chef Art, is a former Iron Chef Thailand renowned for his innovative fusion of Eastern and Western cuisines. At Le Lert, he curates a menu that celebrates Thai culinary traditions, enhanced with contemporary techniques to delight both the eyes and the palate.

Meet Our Chefs

Chef Tempo

​Chef Warakorn Suriyawong, known as Chef Tempo, brings Michelin Guide Bib Gourmand excellence toLe Lert. His culinary style harmoniously blends precision and creativity, honoring traditional Thai flavors while incorporating modern techniques. At Le Lert, Chef Tempo's meticulous attention to detail ensures each dish achieves a delicate balance of flavors and artistic presentation, elevating our culinary offerings.

Must Try Dishes

Dried Khao Soi with Wagyu Beef

​Experience our signature Dried Khao Soi with Wagyu Beef, a delectable combination of stir-fried wide egg wonton noodles in Khao Soi curry paste, tender Wagyu beef slices, green mustard pickles, shallots, crispy noodles, and a side of turmeric soup. It's a culinary experience that embodies the essence of Thai cuisine with a modern twist.

Must Try Dishes

Seafood Tom Yum Fried Rice

Our Seafood Tom Yum Fried Rice is a vibrant blend of tiger prawns, black mussels, scallops, and salmon roe, all enveloped in our signature spicy and tangy tom yum sauce. This dish exemplifies our dedication to delivering authentic Thai flavors with a modern twist.
