Location and Operation

Le Lert is located at 27 Carlton St., Toronto, ON, near College Subway Station and the 506 Streetcar stops at Yonge St. & College St. or Carlton St. & Church St.

We are open every day. Brunch: Monday – Friday | 10 A.M. – 3 P.M. Saturday – Sunday | 9 A.M. – 3 P.M. Dinner: Sunday – Thursday | 5 P.M. – 10 P.M. Friday – Saturday | 5 P.M. – 11 P.M.
