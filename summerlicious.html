Modern Thai Cuisine in Downtown Toronto

Experience Summerlicious with Modern Thai Cuisine in Downtown Toronto

Join Le Lert Thai Cuisine for Summerlicious 2026 and enjoy a specially curated three course lunch or dinner featuring modern Thai flavours crafted with premium ingredients.

Heart of Downtown

Located at 27 Carlton St., Toronto, just steps from College Station, Le Lert offers one of Toronto's most unique Summerlicious experiences, blending authentic Thai inspiration with contemporary presentation.

Whether you're planning a casual lunch, a date night, or dinner with friends, our Summerlicious menu showcases some of our most popular dishes alongside exclusive seasonal creations.

Summerlicious menu

Lunch

------------------------------ Appetizers ------------------------------

Crispy Veggie Spring Rolls

Two golden spring rolls with carrot, green beans, cabbage, ginger, vermicelli, soy protein, and garlic. Served with sweet chili dipping sauce. Vegan, gluten-free.

Fresh Garden Rolls

Two fresh rolls with lettuce, mango, basil, cilantro, pickled carrot, and daikon. Finished with mushroom furikake, garlic-tofu dressing, and peanut sauce. Vegan, gluten-free option, can be nut-free.

Tamarind Glazed Crispy Wings

Crispy chicken wings in tamarind sauce with palm sugar, garlic, and chili. Topped with crispy shallots. Gluten-free option available.

Mango Salad (Yum Ma Muang)

Shredded mango and green apple with red onions, sawtooth coriander, roasted cashews, strawberries, fried shallots, in zesty lime dressing. Vegan, gluten-free, can be nut-free.

------------------------------ Mains ------------------------------

Pad Thai

Rice noodles stir-fried with dried radish, tofu, red onion, bean sprouts, Chinese chives, dried baby shrimp. Peanuts on the side. Available classic (dried baby shrimp), vegan (no egg), or vegetarian (egg ok). Gluten-free option available.

Roasted Chile Prawn Benedict

Tiger prawns sautéed in roasted chili paste on a buttery croissant with poached egg, hollandaise, fresh greens, and balsamic vinaigrette.

Beef Oyster Sauce Rice

Wok-fried beef with onions, mushrooms, and green onions in oyster sauce glaze over jasmine rice with a crispy fried egg.

Chicken Thai Yellow Curry (Gang Garee Gai)

Sliced chicken breast in creamy Thai yellow curry with tri-color potatoes and sweet onions, topped with crispy lotus root chips. Served with jasmine rice and cucumber relish. Vegan option (tofu) and gluten-free option available.

------------------------------ Dessert ------------------------------

Mango Sticky Rice

Rose coconut sticky rice with fresh mango, jasmine-coconut milk sauce, and butterfly pea rice cracker. Vegan, nut-free.

Lychee Sorbet

Lychee-lime sorbet with freeze-dried dragon fruit, fresh pomelo, and lychee. Vegan, gluten-free, nut-free.

Pandan Cake

Pandan layer cake over silky matcha coconut sauce, finished with whipped cream, strawberries, and blueberries. Contains dairy.

Coconut Pudding Mixed Fruit

Silky coconut milk pudding with seasonal mixed fruit. Nut-free.

Summerlicious Menu

Dinner

------------------------------ Appetizers ------------------------------

Crispy Veggie Spring Rolls

Two golden spring rolls with carrot, green beans, cabbage, ginger, vermicelli, soy protein, and garlic. Served with sweet chili dipping sauce. Vegan, gluten-free.

Spiced Thai Beef Tartare

Beef tartare seasoned with roasted rice powder, lemongrass, makrut lime leaves, red onion, mint, herbs, garlic, and egg yolk. Served with jeaw sauce. Mild spicy, gluten-free option available.

Crispy Shrimp Toast

Golden crispy shrimp toast with cucumber relish, shallots, and red chili in delicate Thai-style pickle dressing. Savory, crisp, and bright acidity.

Citrus Miang Kham Salad

Pomelo, romaine lettuce, roasted cashews, toasted coconut, lime, ginger, sweet peppers, red onions, and Asian pear in miso-soy coconut dressing. Vegan, contains nuts.

------------------------------ Mains ------------------------------

Thai Wok-Fried Glass Noodles (Pad Woon Sen)

Glass noodles wok-fried with garlic, egg, onion, wood ear mushrooms, and green onions in savory soy sauce. Choice of chicken or beef.

Seafood Holy Basil Rice

Tiger shrimp, squid, and mussels wok-fried with Thai holy basil, garlic, and chili over jasmine rice with a crispy-edged fried egg. Vegan option available (tofu and mushroom). Mild spicy.

Duck Confit Red Curry

Duck confit simmered in aromatic red curry with Thai eggplant, pea eggplant, tomatoes, lychee, pineapple, Thai basil, and makrut lime leaves. Served with jasmine rice. Mild spicy.

Crispy Seabass with Three Flavor Tamarind Glaze

Crispy seabass fillet glazed with balanced tamarind chili sauce, finished with shallots and fresh herbs. Served with jasmine rice. Gluten-free option available.

------------------------------ Desserts ------------------------------

Mango Sticky Rice

Rose coconut sticky rice with fresh mango, jasmine-coconut milk sauce, and butterfly pea rice cracker. Vegan, nut-free.

Lychee Sorbet

Lychee-lime sorbet with freeze-dried dragon fruit, fresh pomelo, and lychee. Vegan, gluten-free, nut-free.

Thai Tea Cake

Thai tea sponge cake layered with milk-whipped cream, brown butter cornflake crumble, and toasted hazelnuts. Contains hazelnuts, dairy. Nut-free option available.

Crispy Fried Vanilla Ice Cream

Vanilla ice cream in a golden cornflake and breadcrumb crust, lightly fried. Served with whipped cream and warm chocolate sauce. Contains dairy.
